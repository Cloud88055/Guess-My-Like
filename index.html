Our Product

Guess My Like

Guess My Like is a social game developed by CloudForm where players try to guess who among them liked a specific YouTube video or Spotify track.

To enable certain features of the game, users may sign in with Google. In those cases, Google authentication and authorized Google data are used only as necessary to provide the app’s core functionality.

Any Google user data accessed by Guess My Like is used solely for the functionality requested by the user and is not sold or shared with third parties.
